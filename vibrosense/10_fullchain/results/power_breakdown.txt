--- Power Observations ---

1. Envelope detectors dominate (77%) due to the OTA bias currents
   in the peak detector and LPF stages.
2. Power is data-independent: <0.1% variation across 4 test cases.
3. BPF power is low (4.7 uW each) due to Gm-C topology with
   minimal bias current.
4. The 130nm process limits efficiency vs. more advanced nodes,
   but total power is still 5-50x below MCU+FFT approaches.
