--- Power Observations ---

1. BPF ch5 (6 kHz) dominates at 18.7 uW (36.6% of analog chain)
   due to highest Gm requirement for high-frequency operation.

2. BPF ch4 (3 kHz) is second at 9.4 uW. Together ch4+ch5 account
   for 55% of analog power. If application only needs bands 1-3,
   power drops to ~23 uW.

3. Peak detectors are truly passive (diode + RC) — zero DC power.
   This was a 2.7x improvement over the old OTA-based envelope
   detectors that consumed 105.7 uW total (21 uW each).

4. Classifier power revised down from 45 uW estimate to ~5 uW
   based on Block 06's measured charge-domain MAC performance
   (<0.001 uW active, duty-cycled at 10 Hz).

5. System total ~58 uW is competitive with Aspinity AML100 (<180 uW)
   and approaching POLYN's claimed 34 uW.
