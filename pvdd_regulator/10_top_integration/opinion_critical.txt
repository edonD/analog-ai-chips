CRITICAL BUG REPORT — PVDD 5V LDO Regulator
=============================================
Date: 2026-04-02
Analyst: Claude (circuit review)

VERDICT: pass_off polarity is INVERTED in Block 08 mode_control.
         The gate pullup PFET fights the error amp during normal operation,
         destroying loop gain, PSRR, load regulation, and stability.

=========================================================================
THE BUG
=========================================================================

In Block 08 (mode_control), the pass_off output buffer is an INVERTER
driven from comp1b:

  XMpo_bufp pass_off comp1b pvdd pvdd  (PFET, W=4u L=2u)
  XMpo_bufn pass_off comp1b gnd  gnd   (NFET, W=2u L=2u)

This gives: pass_off = NOT(comp1b) = NOT(NOT(comp1)) = comp1.

Tracing the logic:
  BVDD > 2.5V (normal operation):
    tap1 > trip  -->  comp1 = HIGH  -->  comp1b = LOW
    pass_off = NOT(comp1b) = NOT(LOW) = HIGH    <-- WRONG!

  BVDD < 2.5V (POR / startup):
    tap1 < trip  -->  comp1 = LOW  -->  comp1b = HIGH
    pass_off = NOT(comp1b) = NOT(HIGH) = LOW    <-- WRONG!

DESIRED behavior (per top-level design.cir comments):
  pass_off = HIGH when BVDD < 2.5V  (pull gate to BVDD, pass OFF during POR)
  pass_off = LOW  when BVDD > 2.5V  (release gate, let EA regulate)

ACTUAL behavior is exactly opposite. The extra inverter buffer flips polarity.

=========================================================================
HOW THIS DESTROYS THE REGULATOR
=========================================================================

During normal operation (BVDD = 7V, PVDD = 5V), pass_off = HIGH (PVDD level = 5V).

This feeds the BVDD-domain inverter in Block 10:

  XMpo_invp pass_off_b pass_off bvdd bvdd  (PFET W=40u L=2u)
  XMpo_invn pass_off_b pass_off gnd  gnd   (NFET W=2u  L=2u)

The input is pass_off = 5V (PVDD-level from mode_control).
The inverter supply is BVDD = 7V.

Inverter switching threshold ~ 5.0V (due to 20:1 PFET/NFET ratio).
With input AT the threshold, BOTH transistors are ON simultaneously.
Output pass_off_b lands at a MID-RAIL voltage (~3-4V), NOT at BVDD.

Gate pullup PFET:
  XMgate_pu gate pass_off_b bvdd bvdd  (PFET W=4u L=2u)

With pass_off_b ~ 3.5V and BVDD = 7V:
  Vsg = 7 - 3.5 = 3.5V  >>  |Vtp| ~ 0.7V  -->  STRONGLY ON

XMgate_pu conducts ~50-200 uA from BVDD into the gate node continuously.

This current:
  1. Flows backward through Rgate (1k) into the EA output
  2. FIGHTS the EA Stage 2 (which only sources ~20 uA quiescent)
  3. Appears as a low-impedance shunt load on the EA output

Evidence from bias data:
  ea_out = 5.756V, gate = 5.960V  -->  gate is 204mV ABOVE ea_out
  Current through Rgate = 204mV / 1k = 204 uA flowing INTO ea_out
  This 204 uA is the gate pullup current overwhelming the EA.

=========================================================================
THIS SINGLE BUG EXPLAINS ALL FIVE SYMPTOMS
=========================================================================

(1) DC regulation fails above 10mA, collapses to 2.1V at 22mA:
    The EA must pull the gate LOW to deliver more current. But XMgate_pu
    pushes gate HIGH with ~200 uA. The EA Stage 2 (20 uA bias) cannot
    win the tug-of-war. Above ~10mA the gate can't go low enough,
    PVDD drops, and once it drops, the PVDD-powered mode_control
    degrades further (cascading failure).

    Non-monotonic recovery at 40mA: as PVDD collapses, mode_control
    (PVDD-powered) loses its supply, pass_off output degrades toward 0V,
    the pullup partially releases, and the EA partially recovers control.

(2) PSRR is -7dB, not -60dB:
    The gate pullup creates a direct BVDD-to-gate path through the
    partially-ON XMgate_pu. Any BVDD ripple couples straight to the
    gate and through the pass device to PVDD. PSRR = (Rds_on_pass) /
    (Rds_on_pass + Rds_on_pullup) ~ -7dB.

(3) Loop gain is only 20dB (should be 60-80dB):
    The shunt current from XMgate_pu (~200 uA) collapses the effective
    output impedance of the EA. Gain = gm x Rout, but Rout is now
    dominated by Rgate (1k) in series with the pullup's low Rds,
    instead of the EA's intrinsic MegaOhm output impedance.
    20dB is consistent with the EA driving into a ~1k load.

(4) Gate oscillates during startup:
    During startup, pass_off = HIGH (inverted, should be LOW).
    The pullup PFET fights the EA output continuously. The EA tries to
    pull gate low to charge the output cap, the pullup pushes it back,
    creating limit-cycle oscillation at ~hundreds of Hz. The oscillation
    damps out only when soft-start (tau=2.2ms) completes and the loop
    reaches a quasi-stable compromise.

(5) FS 150C corner is unstable:
    At fast corner + 150C: lower Vtp --> XMgate_pu conducts even MORE
    at the same Vsg. The pullup current increases, further degrading
    phase margin. The system crosses from "marginally stable" to
    "oscillating."

=========================================================================
THE FIX
=========================================================================

File: ../08_mode_control/design.cir, lines 75-76

CURRENT (BROKEN):
  XMpo_bufp pass_off comp1b pvdd pvdd sky130_fd_pr__pfet_g5v0d10v5 w=4e-6 l=2e-6
  XMpo_bufn pass_off comp1b gnd  gnd  sky130_fd_pr__nfet_g5v0d10v5 w=2e-6 l=2e-6

FIX (change comp1b --> comp1 as the buffer input):
  XMpo_bufp pass_off comp1 pvdd pvdd sky130_fd_pr__pfet_g5v0d10v5 w=4e-6 l=2e-6
  XMpo_bufn pass_off comp1 gnd  gnd  sky130_fd_pr__nfet_g5v0d10v5 w=2e-6 l=2e-6

This makes: pass_off = NOT(comp1)
  BVDD < 2.5V: comp1=LOW  --> pass_off=HIGH  --> pullup ON (POR protection) CORRECT
  BVDD > 2.5V: comp1=HIGH --> pass_off=LOW   --> pullup OFF (EA controls)  CORRECT

One-token change: "comp1b" --> "comp1" in two lines.

=========================================================================
EXPECTED RESULTS AFTER FIX
=========================================================================

With pass_off = LOW during normal operation:
  - pass_off_b = BVDD (inverter output HIGH)
  - XMgate_pu: Vsg = 0V --> fully OFF, zero current into gate
  - EA output drives gate with full open-loop gain (60-80dB expected)
  - DC regulation holds to 50mA+ (pass device has 1mm width)
  - PSRR improves to -50 to -60dB (no BVDD-to-gate feedthrough)
  - Loop gain rises to 60-80dB
  - Gate oscillation during startup eliminated
  - FS 150C corner stabilized (pullup no longer degrades phase margin)

=========================================================================
WHY THIS BUG SURVIVED
=========================================================================

1. Light-load testing masked it: At 1mA, the EA has enough margin to
   fight the 200uA pullup and still regulate (just barely). PVT sweeps
   were done at 1mA, showing 4.993-4.998V -- looks perfect.

2. The v11 fix (FIX-14: wider inverter, weaker pullup) was treating the
   SYMPTOM, not the cause. Making the pullup weaker reduces the fighting
   current, but doesn't fix the polarity. The pullup is still partially
   ON when it should be fully OFF.

3. The "60/60 PVT PASS" was achieved at light load where the bug has
   minimal impact. Heavy-load and AC tests reveal the truth.
